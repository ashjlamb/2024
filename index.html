Living Off the Grid
Home Essentials Essentials Cont.
Living off the grid is not at easy as you might think.
It entails more than just raising some livestock,
starting a garden, and hanging out on your porch swing.
It takes planning, preparation, and a lot of work to maintain your homestead.
The other two pages on this website will give you an idea on some
of the essentials you will need to plan for in going off the grid.
