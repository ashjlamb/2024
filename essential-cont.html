Living Off the Grid
Home Essentials Essentials Cont.

There are many essentials required in going off the grid. Lets look at some of those essentials:

Power Generation System
Off grid power is where the power is generated by yourself in one way or another. This means having a power generation system with backups that
can run all of your eletricity you need to run all of you electrical items. There are several renewable power energy options. The two most popular
are solar and wind.

Power Storage System:
During the day you will be generating energy, but at night or if the wind stops blowing you will need somewhere to have stored energy. You will need to
purchase batteries that are intended for this use. They are not cheap. You will also need a battery bank and storage system, witha charge controller, inverter, etc.

Heating and Cooling:
Heating and cooling the off grid home will tie into your power generation and are essential things to consider when planning. A wood stove is a popular off grid
option for heating and cooking. Passive heating and cooling is a great way to regulare the temperature of your off grid home without draining your power source.

Waste Disposal and Septic System:
You will need some sort of composting waste disposal system or a traditional septic system. Composting toilets are highly efficient and therefore are a great investment.
